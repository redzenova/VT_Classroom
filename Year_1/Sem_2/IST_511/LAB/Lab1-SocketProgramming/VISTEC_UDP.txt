VISION

VISTEC’s vision is to become a world-class frontier science and innovation hub in Thailand


MISSION

VISTEC’s mission is to provide a world-class research and education platform for creating excellent science and impactful innovations for industry, society and environment:

• Conducting cutting-edge research to enhance Thailand’s competitiveness in new discoveries and development of proprietary innovations
• Enabling close research collaborations and linkages between talented researchers and industry
• Fostering entrepreneurship opportunities: Creating the path from research to usable innovations to commercially viable and scalable products.
